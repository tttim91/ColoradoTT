Training Material

  • Websites
    • Table Tennis Coaching - Tons of articles, tips, links and information
    • Ping Skills - Videos explaining all strokes and techniques (some videos need membership to watch though)
    • Table Tennis DB - Database of all table tennis information including amazing amount of videos in video section
    • TT Edge - Great video section with explanations of all strokes and techniques
    • Expert Table Tennis - Interesting "Learn table tennis in 10 days" training approach
  • Youtube Channels
    • Official ITTF Channel - Over 4000 videos including table tennis podcast (pongcast), tournament videos, "off the table" videos and many, many more
    • Official USATT Channel - Mostly videos of USATT tournaments including US Open, US Nationals, ect...
    • Dynamic Table Tennis - Coach Brian Pace videos showing technique, tactics, strategies and some interviews
    • Janus 770 - Tons of short form tournament matches from most world-class tournaments
    • TT Countenance - Another channel with tons of short form tournament matches (Very high quality)
    • Info TT - Montage videos and other fun table tennis videos to watch including "best of 2015" and "best of 2014"
  • Books
    • Table Tennis Tactics for Thinkers - Hall of Fame Coach Larry Hodges take on table tennis tactics (how to win)
    • ITTF Advanced Coaching Manual - Mostly for coaches use, but includes all techniques, strategies and training concepts to use for self coaching
    • Table Tennis Steps to Success - Coach Richard McAfee of SkyHigh discusses table tennis technique for all strokes
    • 100 Days of Table Tennis - Coaches Sampson Dubina and Larry Hodges give 100 days worth of table tennis advice
    • Expert in a Year Challenge - Describes the journey of a player from absolute beginner to proficient player while teaching about technique and tactics
